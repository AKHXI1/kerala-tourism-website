About Kerala

Kerala, located on the southwestern coast of India, is famous for its natural beauty, serene backwaters, lush green hill stations, rich wildlife, and vibrant traditions. It is one of the most popular tourist destinations in India, offering a perfect mix of relaxation and adventure.

Top Destinations

Backwaters

Enjoy a houseboat cruise in Alappuzha and Kumarakom.

Munnar Hills

Experience tea plantations and breathtaking views.

Beaches

Relax at Kovalam, Varkala, and Cherai beaches.

Wildlife

Explore Periyar & Thekkady rich with flora and fauna.
