Majestic Waterfalls of Kerala

Athirappilly Waterfalls

Known as the "Niagara of India", Athirappilly is the largest waterfall in Kerala, cascading from a height of 80 feet amidst lush greenery.

Meenmutty Waterfalls

Located in Wayanad, Meenmutty is a three-tiered waterfall dropping from nearly 1,000 feet, offering breathtaking trekking trails.

Soochipara Waterfalls

Soochipara, also called Sentinel Rock Falls, is a 200m high waterfall in Wayanad surrounded by dense forest and rock climbing spots.

Palaruvi Waterfalls

Meaning "Stream of Milk", Palaruvi is a stunning 300-foot waterfall in Kollam district, set amidst rich forests and Ayurvedic heritage.
