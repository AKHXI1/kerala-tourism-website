Famous Islands in Kerala

Willingdon Island

An artificial island in Kochi, home to luxury hotels, offices, and naval base.

Vypin Island

Connected to Kochi by bridges and ferries, known for Cherai Beach and fishing villages.

Pathiramanal

A small island in Vembanad Lake, a haven for migratory birds and nature lovers.

Munroe Island

Located in Kollam, famous for canal cruises, coir making, and traditional village life.

Bolgatty Island

Known for the historic Dutch palace and scenic views of Kochi backwaters.
